FOUR FACTORS

UPSIDE SCORE: 7/10
“Can this team explode?”

Measures big-play ability, breakout potential, high-ceiling players, and stack/tier-breaking combos.

RELIABILITY SCORE: 7/10
“Can I count on this roster?”

Focuses on injury history, consistency, role security, and number of plug-and-play guys.

DEPTH SCORE: 7/10
“Will this team survive?”

Looks at viable backups, flex options, bench upside, and bye-week coverage.

RISK PROFILE: 7/10
“Will this roster implode?”

Accounts for volatility, roster construction flaws, overreliance on fragile players, or all-in builds.
